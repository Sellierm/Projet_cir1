Together we created Cr0ss Math3matic, a game of reflection and mental calculation. Here are the rules to gain a better understanding of the game : The Rules

You have different options at hand :

In the Training mode, you will be able to create your own personalized level in order to become greater by choosing whatever parameters that suits you (different operations signs, bigger or smaller grid size, etc).


Training


In the Campaign mode, we have imagined a fun way of learning and discovering the regions of France by going through an interactive map that represent the different levels of the game.


Campaign


Finally we have the Competitive mode. Register yourself or log in if the registration is already done. And try to beat the time records of other players around the world in order to become the best Cross Mathematician !

Good luck!
